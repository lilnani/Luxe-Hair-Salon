Our Experience

Mastery refined
over fifteen years

Founded by master stylist Isabelle Laurent, Lumière Studio was built on a simple belief: every client deserves a transformative, personalised experience. We've trained under global hair artisans in Paris, London, and New York — bringing that world-class expertise to our studio doors.

From one chair to a full team of six specialist stylists, our growth has always been driven by trust, craft, and a relentless pursuit of excellence.

🏆
Award-Winning Stylists Regional & national industry awards since 2014, including Best Salon three years running
🎓
Continuous Education Our team attends global masterclasses and keeps up with the latest techniques every season
🌿
Premium, Ethical Products We use only certified, cruelty-free brands including Olaplex, Redken, and Wella Professionals
